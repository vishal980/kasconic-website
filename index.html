About Kasconic Global

Kasconic is a new age pharmaceutical company with ultra-modern facility to manufacture quality medicines from India. Our mission is to deliver best quality and innovative products to enhance the quality of life for every patient.

Our journey began in 2015, leading to the commissioning of two WHO-GMP approved manufacturing units.

Global Presence Latam America, South East Asia, Africa
2 WHO-GMP Plants Dedicated facilities for various dosage forms

Advanced Manufacturing Excellence

We utilize state-of-the-art technology and processes in our two dedicated manufacturing units to ensure the highest standards of product safety and efficacy.
